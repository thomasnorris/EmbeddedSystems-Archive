Release Notes for TExaS

Installer for Spring 2015 EE319K 

Software for Volume 1. Embedded Systems: Introduction to ARM Cortex M Microcontrollers

Test EXecute, And Simulate, called TExaS, is a collection of DLL plugins that transform Keil uVision into learning environment for embedded systems. These plugins provide simulation of the Texas Instruments Tiva LaunchPad. Furthermore, TExaS can be used for automatic grading of labs both in simulation and on the real board.

TExaS accompanies the books
